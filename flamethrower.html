How It Was Built

Overview

Peter He and I spent the summer designing and building the flamethrower in the basement of Halo, the SF Startup we had joined for the summer

This is the first hand-held flamethrower that connects directly to an onboard butane canister allowing automatically refuel between punches, providing the most entertaining firebending experience yet


I designed and assembled the Electricals and Butante Plumbing of the Flamethrower. The system ran off of a XIAO NRF52840 which controlled the Relays which triggered the Arc Lighter Solenoid valves. The onboard IMU detects the acceleration and quick halt of a punching fist, upon which the flamethrower activated.

The system included many safety mechanisms: An E-Stop, NC-Solenoids, Pressure Release Vents, and Pressure Sensors to monitor the capacity of the flamethrower
